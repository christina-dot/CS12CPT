You can add an event, task or reminder to one of the days on the calendar.

Switching Between Months

  1. At the top left of the calendar there are 2 arrows: back and forward. This allows you to switch between months and years.
  2. If you click the left arrow, the calendar will flip to the previous month.
  3. To advance to the next month click on the right arrow.

Deleting Tasks

When you have completed a task or you want to make changes to the task you can delete it and add a new one.

  1. On the calendar and in the day cell which has an event look for a purple trash can icon.
  2. Press this icon to delete the item from the calendar.
